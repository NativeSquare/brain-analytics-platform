Hi Maxime, here's a list of some of the features that could work well for you guys (no need to deal with the actual football data/models).

1. Calendar & Scheduling
   • Core: Events (Matches, Training, Meetings, Rehab), RSVP tracking, and a "What's on Today" dashboard for club TVs.
   • Admins: Create recurring/one-off events, invite specific groups (First team, Staff, etc.), and track master RSVPs. Updates automatically trigger notifications.
   • Users: Sync with personal Apple/Google Calendars and submit RSVPs (with absence reasons).

2. Document Hub
   • Core: Folders for Playbooks, Policies, Contracts, and Nutrition plans (supports PDFs, Sheets, Images, Video links).
   • Admins: Upload, manage viewing permissions (e.g., private contracts vs. team playbooks), replace outdated files, and track who has opened/read documents.
   • Users: View, download, and search available docs.

3. Player Profiles
   • Core: Bio, Performance Stats, Physical/Fitness Data, Contracts (hidden), and Personal info.
   • Admins: Onboard players, input match/fitness stats, manage contracts, and archive to "Alumni" when sold/released (revokes access).
   • Players: View own stats/contracts. Edit their own contact info, emergency contacts.

4. Staff Profiles & Directory
   • Core: Bios, Job Titles, Certifications (w/ expiry dates), and App Permission levels.
   • Admins: Manage onboarding, set app access levels (e.g., Physio vs. Scout), and receive automated alerts when staff certs/safeguarding expire in 30 days.
   • Staff: Update personal details and upload renewed licenses/badges.
   • Players: "View-only" access to use as a club directory (e.g., finding the Physio or Kit Manager's phone number).

5. Injury Reporting
   • Core: Injury logs (w/ Orchard Codes), Mechanism of Injury, timelines, and color-coded status (Red: Out, Yellow: Modified, Green: Cleared).
   • Medical/Admins: Log injuries instantly, update rehab notes/status, manage return dates, and archive closed reports.

6. Scouting Reports
   • Core: Target info, grading, media, and final recommendations (Sign/Watch/Pass) - need to check with Jesper format of scout reports.
   • Scouts/Admins: Draft reports on the go (mobile-friendly UI), add subsequent notes, and archive dead targets.
   • Users: Restricted (No access).

7. Scouting Shadow Teams
   • Core: Visual pitch view ranking targets by position (Immediate, Development, Emergency/Loan).
   • Scouts/Admins: Add players from scouting reports directly to the Shadow Team and drag-and-drop to re-rank based on form/value.

8. W\*\*\*\*App Notifications
   • Core: Automated triggers for new events, schedule changes, and reminders.
   • Admins: Send manual broadcasts (e.g., "Training canceled"), manage auto-message templates, and update broadcast lists.
   • Users: Opt-in/out of alerts (for privacy compliance).

These are ranked by priority. Please have a look and tell us what you think could be achieved in a 2 week sprint. Let me know if you need more information about some of these features to draft your proposal.

Thanks!
